Product Overview

What is Startup Value Navigator?

Startup Value Navigator is a professional-grade, web-based startup valuation platform that combines VC-backed methodologies, real-time calculations, and AI-powered insights to help founders, investors, and advisors estimate enterprise value with confidence.

Unlike generic calculators, our platform applies stage-appropriate methodologies‚ÄîBerkus Method for pre-revenue startups, Scorecard for seed, and Revenue Multiples for growth-stage companies‚Äîensuring realistic, market-aligned valuations.

Valuation Methodology

Our valuation engine uses four industry-standard methodologies, automatically selecting the most appropriate based on your stage and revenue.

Berkus Method

Pre-seed / Concept

Assigns up to $500K per risk factor: Sound Idea, Prototype, Team Quality, Strategic Relationships, and Market Timing. Maximum pre-money: $2.5M.

Scorecard Method

Seed Stage

Compares your startup to regional averages across weighted factors: Team (30%), Market (25%), Product (15%), Competition (10%), and more.

Revenue Multiple

Series A+

Applies growth-adjusted ARR multiples: 10-25x for Series A, 8-18x for Series B, 6-15x for Series C. Adjusted for margins, retention, and burn.

First Chicago

All Stages

Probability-weighted scenarios (Bear/Base/Bull) provide a realistic range rather than a single point estimate.

üìö Research-Backed

All methodologies are sourced from academic literature and VC industry practices. See our detailed VALUATION_METHODOLOGY.md document for full citations and validation against market data.
